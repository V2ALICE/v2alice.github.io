Dreaming

A wise man from house 9 once said, "learning is incomplete if fun is not accompanied". The club takes this seriously, and so having fun will be a default in the club to not only keep up the motivation but also the creativity levels. On weekly basis we will create a system or a program just for the sake of fun, an example can be a bot that send jokes to the RBC groupchat 24/7 or a simply the most useless system in the human history.

We will also explore the aesthetics of technology, creating visually or sensory appealing/interactive stuff and even more, unlimited possibilies
