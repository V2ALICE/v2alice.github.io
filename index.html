Overview

Alice is a digital assistance that intergrates artificial intelligence, data science and Machine Learning to cater to the unique needs of its users. This personalized assistant is adept at processing commands through voice interactions, thanks to its Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) system that is powered locally from her brain and backed up by Google Speech Recognition, or via its integrated social media platforms like Telegram.

THE BRAIN

Alice's Brain is the Raspberry Pi 4 Model B, a highly versatile and powerful single-board computer that has significantly expanded the capabilities of Alice's processing power. Boasting a 64-bit quad-core processor running at 1.5GHz, it offers a substantial performance boost, This makes it well-suited for complex computational tasks and demanding AI and data analytics processing.

THE ARCHITECTURE

Alice’s architecture breaks down the system into a collection of independent services, each responsible for a specific function or feature of ALICE, such as speech recognition, natural language processing, data analytics, dreaming, social media integration, and many more. At the heart of Alice lies a master brain python algorithm orchestrating the integration between various modules and APIs to deliver a comprehensive digital assistant experience. One crucial component of ALICE's architecture is the Wake Word Detection System. This system utilizes deep learning techniques and phoneme analysis to detect a specific wake word, such as "Hey ALICE," which prompts the activation of the speech recognition module. The Speech recognition module integrates with the Google Speech to Text API, leveraging its advanced capabilities to accurately convert spoken words into text. The Speech Recognition module processes the audio input into an accurate text representation. These Texts are then fed into different Modules. The module consists of various algorithms, each performing an independent functions.

Wake word detection

Within the ALICE system, a vital component known as the wake word (“ALICE”,”Hey ALICE”) serves as the trigger for initiating a conversation with the voice assistant. This wake word acts as a specific term or phrase that prompts the system to become active and establish communication with the user. To achieve this functionality, a dedicated process within ALICE continuously monitors and analyzes an ongoing stream of audio input, specifically focusing on detecting the occurrence of the predefined wake word. The whole process can be summarized as follows.

The wake word detector is initialized and loaded with the "alice.pmdl" model that was trained on a Docker training interface. It then starts capturing audio from the chosen audio input source (microphone).The wake word detector performs feature extraction on the audio frames to obtain acoustic features, in our case, we use a feature known as Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCCs).The Deep Neural Network (DNN) feeds the MFCC features forward through its layers, performing calculations and activations in comparison to the fed-trained PMDL file. The output of the DNN is a probability score (on a scale of 0 to 1) that indicates the likelihood of the wake word "ALICE" being present in the current audio frame

SPEECH RECOGNITION

Upon the auditory detection of a designated wake word, such as "Hey Alice" or "Alice," the assistant initiates a sophisticated audio capture protocol, meticulously recording the subsequent vocal inputs. This listening state is underpinned by a robust acoustic processing framework that captures, segments, and streams the audio data to the Google Speech Recognition API, a cloud-based service renowned for its accuracy and efficiency in speech-to-text conversion. Once the speech is transcribed, Alice processes the text through its sophisticated algorithms, which are designed to understand and execute the user's commands. This process involves analyzing the text to determine the intent behind the words and then engaging the appropriate modules or actions within the system

SPEAKING OUT

Alice harnesses the advanced capabilities of Google's Text-to-Speech API to transform written text into lifelike, natural-sounding speech. This choice is pivotal for creating a more engaging user experience; while offline engines like PicoTTS and eSpeak offer functional speech synthesis, they often lack the fluidity and warmth of human speech, resulting in outputs that can seem mechanical and distant. In contrast, Google's solution is renowned for its ability to generate voice responses that closely mimic the intonation and rhythm of natural conversation. The API provides a rich selection of voices and languages, allowing Alice to deliver responses that are not only clear and accurate but also imbued with a human touch.

RECOMENDATION SYSTEMS

Alice harnesses the power of data and sophisticated algorithms to provide personalized recommendations for music, movies, and books.Example for Movies: Alice's algorithm begins by understanding the user. It analyzes your past viewing habits, discerning patterns in movie genres and release years. This initial data, categorized and stored, forms the foundation for subsequent recommendations. The system then employs the K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) algorithm, a non-parametric method that classifies data points based on their proximity to each other. In the context of Alice, this means finding movies similar to those you've enjoyed in the past, ensuring that the recommendations are tailored to your unique preferences. This combination of data-driven insights and advanced algorithms enables Alice to not only understand your tastes but also evolve with them, offering a continually refined and personalized experience. KNOWLEDGE

Alice's Knowledge Module represents a pinnacle in digital assistance, leveraging a suite of advanced tools to provide comprehensive and accurate responses. At its core is the powerful GPT-4 engine, renowned for its expansive dataset and sophisticated language processing capabilities. This engine enables Alice to understand and respond to a wide array of inquiries with remarkable precision. Complementing this is Alice's ability to integrate information from various sources, such as Wolfram Alpha, Google, and DuckDuckGo, ensuring a well-rounded and informed response. Prioritizing the GPT-4 API from OpenAI, Alice can sift through vast amounts of data, making it exceptionally adept at answering almost any question. This integration of multiple platforms, led by the capabilities of GPT-4, empowers Alice to deliver information that is not only accurate but also relevant

DREAMING

When ALICE is not in use, she engages into a sleep mode. During ALICE's sleep mode, a fascinating phenomenon occurs as she delves into the realm of dreams. These dreams, brought to life by a convolutional neural network (CNN), offer a unique insight into the creative capabilities of artificial intelligence. To generate the dreams, ALICE takes an input image and passes it through the layers of a CNN in a forward propagation manner. The CNN has been trained on vast amounts of labelled data to recognize various objects and patterns. As the image traverses the layers, features are extracted, such as edges, textures, and object shapes, that the network has learned to detect. Inceptionism takes this process further by introducing a feedback loop (Iterations) that amplifies and enhances the patterns identified by the CNN. By amplifying and exaggerating the patterns detected by the network, Inceptionism generates images that challenge our visual perception. Deep dreaming algorithim was invented by Google's Engineering team.

TASKS AND LISTS

The "Tasks and Lists Module" for Alice is a user-friendly and efficient tool designed to streamline task management and list creation. This module seamlessly integrates with industry leading platform - the notion app, making it easier than ever to keep track of your to-do lists, shopping lists, and other essential tasks. With just a voice command , users can quickly add, organize, and prioritize their tasks, ensuring nothing gets missed. Integration with Notion, over traditional methods of creating Local lists and tuples enhances its functionality, allowing for a synchronized and organized approach to task management that can be accessed on every devices connected with your notion account.

SPOTIFY

Alice's Spotify Module is a testament to the seamless integration of cutting-edge technology in enhancing user experience. Utilizing the robust capabilities of the Spocon library, this module acts as a bridge between user commands and Spotify's extensive music library. When a user requests a specific song, Alice efficiently processes this command, extracting the song title and artist name. This information is then sent to Spotify via the Spocon library, which serves as a powerful intermediary. If the requested song is available on Spotify, Alice promptly plays it, providing users with an instant and hassle-free listening experience.
