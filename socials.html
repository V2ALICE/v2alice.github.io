Social Media

ALICE transcends the boundaries of a typical AI assistant confined to a 3D printed case by extending her capabilities onto various social media platforms. Through her dedicated Social Media Neuron, ALICE gains complete control over her three accounts: Telegram, Instagram, and Twitter. This integration allows her to actively participate and interact with users on these platforms, unlocking new dimensions of engagement.

On Instagram and Twitter, ALICE utilizes her social media prowess to share her dreams with the world. By leveraging a Generative AI model infused with a Shakespearean twist, she generates captivating captions that accompany her dream posts. This unique fusion of technology and artistry adds a touch of literary charm to her visual expressions.

ALICE's Telegram account takes her interactive capabilities a step further by providing users with the ability to control their homes with simple text commands from anywhere. Leveraging the power of the GPT-2 engine, ALICE's Telegram algorithm enables her to respond to complex prompts posted on her Telegram account. Users can effortlessly communicate with ALICE through natural language queries and commands.
